# AidChain: A Decentralized Aid Distribution Platform

Welcome to AidChain, an innovative Decentralized Application (DApp) focused on revolutionizing the charitable donation and aid distribution process. AidChain ensures transparency, accountability, and efficiency in the world of philanthropy. Below are the key features and instructions on how to get started with AidChain.

## Features

- **User Registration and Onboarding**: Seamlessly onboard users, ensuring a smooth experience.
- **Donations and Contribution**: Accept contributions in various cryptocurrencies, promoting inclusivity.
- **Aid Distribution**: Transparent and automated aid distribution for efficient humanitarian efforts.
- **Real-Time Tracking and Reporting**: Instant tracking of donations and aid distribution for utmost transparency.
- **Decentralized Verification and Reputation**: Trustworthy verification processes to ensure authenticity.
- **Feedback and Testimonials**: Empower beneficiaries to share their experiences, building trust.
- **NFT-based Fundraising and Memorabilia**: Unique digital collectibles for impactful fundraising.
- **Community Governance**: Inclusive decision-making through decentralized governance mechanisms.
- **Multi-Language Support**: Reach a broader audience by offering the platform in multiple languages.
- **Smart Contracts for Fundraising Events**: Host diverse fundraising activities through smart contracts.
- **Privacy-Preserving Donation Options**: Anonymity-focused features, ensuring donor privacy.
- **Smart Impact Metrics**: Measure and showcase the tangible impact of donations for transparency.
- **Inclusive Payment Options**: Accept payments via various methods, promoting financial inclusivity.
- **Disaster Relief and Emergency Funds**: Rapid response for natural disasters and unforeseen crises.

## Getting Started

### Prerequisites

- **Node.js and npm**
- **Expo CLI**
- **WalletConnect Provider**
- **Wallet compatible with EVM chains**
- **Solidity Smart Contracts**

### Installation

1. **Clone the repository**: `git clone https://github.com/engrusmanbelloa/aidchain.git`
2. **Navigate to the project directory**: `cd aidchain`
3. **Install dependencies**: `yarn install`
4. **Configure WalletConnect provider and wallet addresses** in the code.

### Usage

1. **Start the Expo development server**: `yarn start`
2. **Scan the QR code** with your Expo Go app on your mobile device.
3. **Follow on-screen instructions** to register, connect your wallet, and explore the features.
